Trivago

With trivago you can easily find your ideal hotel and compare prices from different websites.
Just enter the place you want to visit and the dates you want,
and let the trivago search engine compare prices for available hotel accommodation for you. To refine your search results, you can use filters by price,
distance (from the city center, for example), number of stars or facilities, and more. If you want to book a room in a small hotel or a suite in a luxury hotel,
trivago makes it easy for you to book online. You can search through a large variety of rooms and accommodations in the Arab world such as Cairo, Tunis, Hammamet,
as well as the most popular cities and favorite vacation destinations abroad!
